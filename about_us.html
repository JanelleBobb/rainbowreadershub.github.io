About Us

Rainbow Readers’ Book Hub is an online book library which focuses on the educational advancement of our nations’ young adults. Our goal as a library, is to locate a variety of text(s) to meet the educational, business, and spiritual needs of those who seek to enhance their personal development as well as their academic career. Another focal point of our online library is to improve the literacy of our young adults which is fundamental to their all-round development.

The addition of our novels collection, will stimulate and encourage our young adults enact on their reading journey as well as being motivated to become prospective innovative writers. As we continue to embark on this journey, our main initiative is to expand our network by collaborating with other libraries, locally and internationally, for the ease of accessibility of the different genres of books.

Therefore, we welcome you to our website where your reading and academic journey can take fruition.
