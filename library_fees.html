Registration Fees

  • TTD$5.00 One-time payment (Nationals of Trinidad and Tobago)
  • TTD$10.00 One-time payment (non Nationals)
  • Free (Senior Citizens >60 years)

Overdue Fees

Hard copy/paperback

  • Books held 5 days after due date: TTD$2.50
  • Books held 10 days after due date: TTD$5.00
  • Books held 1 month after due date: TTD$10.00

eBooks/PDF

eBooks can be renewed for a period of three (3) days maximum after your first due date.

Please note if you attempt to renew ebook(s) for the third time, you are required to wait three (3) business days before you attempt to access the ebook(s).
